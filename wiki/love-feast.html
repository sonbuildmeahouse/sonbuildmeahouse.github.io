Traditions

Here are the traditions surrounding contributions to the love feast.

  1. There will be no monetization/fund for shabbat feast contributions.
  2. Partaking in the love feast is an opportunity to practice being hospitable and fulfill Hebrews 13. So be in the pattern of being mindful to contribute dishes and food to the love feast if it is in your hand to do it.
  3. Another practice that we encourage is to find someone who is spiritually gifted in hospitality/service OR imitable through maturity in hospitality/service to prepare something in your stead, and then give that person alms.
