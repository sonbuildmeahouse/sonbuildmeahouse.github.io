Hermeneutics & Application

Tradition

We have diverse traditions based on our exegesis of the above doctrine. :

  1. You can literally "bring a psalm" as a contribution. This can look like just opening your bible and reading a psalm to the body.
  2. You can bring a prepared and/or well-known psalm/hymn and sing this at the gathering. You can sing alone or lead the body in the song.
  3. Under inspiration from the Spirit, you can break into a song as led by Spirit; this would just be for the body's reception (although, it would be really cool if the Holy Spirit put the same song into multiple people's hearts.
